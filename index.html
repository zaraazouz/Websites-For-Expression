Stuart Gibbs

Stuart Gibbs is by far my favorite author. His best series is the Spy School Series, but his other ones are the FunJungle Series, the MBA (Moon Base Alpha) Series, and the Last Musketeer Series. (I haven't read the Last Musketeer books, but I heard that they're good.)

MUST READ Stuart Gibbs Books

  • ALL SPY SCHOOL BOOKS:
    1. Spy School
    2. Spy Camp
    3. Evil Spy School
    4. Spy Ski School
    5. Spy School Secret Service
  • Panda-Monium (It's the fourth book in the FunJungle series. The other books are good, but this one is the best.)
  • Space Case (First book in the MBA series. The second book is okay, but this one is better.)
